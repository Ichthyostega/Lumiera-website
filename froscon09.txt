Lumiera at FrOSCon 2009!!
=========================

image:FrOSCon09/froscon.png[FrOSCon logo]

The conference
--------------

On August 22-23 2009 the Lumiera community participated to FrOSCon, +
the 4th German conference on Free Software and Open Source held at the University of Applied Sciences Bonn-Rhine-Sieg.

image:FrOSCon09/entrance.png["The FrOSCon venue", width=180, link="entrance.png"] image:FrOSCon09/booth.png["The Lumiera booth", width=180, link="booth.png"] image:FrOSCon09/entrance2.png["The FrOSCon venue", width=180, link="entrance2.png"]

The project
-----------

6 community members had fun meeting interesting people and projects, discussing about video editing and openvideo, presenting the Lumiera project, its goals and its plans.

------------

image:FrOSCon09/RadioTux_Logo.png["Radio tux logo", width=180, link="RadioTux_Logo.png"] image:FrOSCon09/jalalbad-school-with-linux-olpc-lxde-freifunk.png["Mario Behling", width=180, link="jalalbad-school-with-linux-olpc-lxde-freifunk.png"] image:FrOSCon09/blue-1024x768.thumbnail.png["LXDE screenshot", width=180, link="blue-1024x768.thumbnail.png"] 

Mario Behling from http://www.lxde.org/[LXDE] talked to the Lumiera team in front of a microphone for 35 minutes. + 
The interview is now online at Mario's blog http://www.perspektive89.com/2009/lumiera_radio_interview_during_froscon_2009[perspective89] and at http://blog.radiotux.de/2009/09/02/interview-ueber-lumiera/[Radio Tux]. 

Thanks to Mario for his warm support and to the Radio Tux team who made this happen. 

Video production and education
------------------------------

image:FrOSCon09/skolelinux.png["Skolelinux logo", width=180, link="skolelinux.png"] image:FrOSCon09/mascotte.png["Skolelinux Mascotte", width=100, link="mascotte.png"] image:FrOSCon09/stopmotion.png["Stopmotion logo", width=200, link="stopmotion.png"]

During the conference, Skolelinux and Lumiera teams discussed and experimented possible uses of video for educational purposes. + 
The fun included a Stopmotion workshop for kids and teachers.

A short video
-------------

image:FrOSCon09/frame2.png["Frame from the video", width=180, link="frame2.png"] image:FrOSCon09/frame1.png["Frame from the video", width=180, link="frame1.png"] image:FrOSCon09/frame3.png["Frame from the video", width=180, link="frame3.png"]


Playing with a camera and a steadicam we ended up shooting some footage. + 
We are now editing it in Cinelerra to produce a short video that won't tell you anything interesting about the conference, nor about our project or any FOSS related stuff. 

This "about nothing" video will be on-line as soon as time permits.
