== FileMapCache
:Author: MichaelPloujnikov
:Date Created: 200706202207
:Date Changed: 200706270305
:Count Changes: 4

The +FileMapCache+ keeps a list of <<backend-FileMap, +FileMap+>>s which are currently not in use and subject of aging.  Whenever a +FileMap+ is in use, it is checked out into an in-use list where it is not subject to aging.
